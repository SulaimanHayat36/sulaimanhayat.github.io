Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 24 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 3/4 cup
Granulated sugar 3/4 cup
Brown sugar 3/4 cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
All-purpose flour 2 1/4 cups
Baking soda 1 teaspoon
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Semi-sweet chocolate chips 2 cups

Step 1: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).

Step 2: In a large bowl, cream together the butter, granulated sugar and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

Step 3: In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda and salt. Gradually stir into the creamed mixture. Fold in the chocolate chips.

Step 4: Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets, leaving some space between them.

Step 5: Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the edges are golden. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.

Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 12 oz
Ground beef 1 lb
Onion 1, chopped
Garlic 2 cloves, minced
Tomato sauce 2 cups
Oregano 1 teaspoon
Basil 1 teaspoon
Salt and pepper To taste
Parmesan cheese To serve

Step 1: Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water, according to the package directions, until al dente. Drain and return to the pot.

Step 2: In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook the ground beef, breaking it up with a spatula, until browned and cooked through. Drain the excess fat.

Step 3: Add the onion and garlic to the same skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, until soft and fragrant, about 10 minutes.

Step 4: Stir in the tomato sauce, oregano, basil, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer, uncovered, for about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Step 5: Serve the spaghetti topped with the bolognese sauce and sprinkled with parmesan cheese.
